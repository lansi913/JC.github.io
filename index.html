About Me

I am currently a Ph.D. candidate at Shanghai Jiao Tong University. My research focuses on biomedical engineering, specifically in the area of contactless cardiopulmonary monitoring using microwave and millimeter-wave radar systems.

I am particularly interested in bridging hardware design and advanced data processing, utilizing deep learning and machine learning architectures (such as CNNs and Transformers) to extract reliable physiological signals from radar data. My work also involves scientific programming in Python (NumPy, SciPy, Pandas) for digital filtering, data alignment, and signal modulation.
